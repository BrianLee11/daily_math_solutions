Solve the equation

$$
x+5=14-\frac{1}{2} x
$$

Notes

1) Solve the equation $\approx$ find $x$ such that satisfies the relationship $x+5=14-\frac{1}{2} x$
2) $x+5=14-\frac{1}{2} x \quad$ [original equation]

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \Rightarrow x+5+\frac{1}{2} x=14-\frac{1}{2} x+\frac{1}{2} x[\because ? ? \text { property }] \\
& \Rightarrow x+5+\frac{1}{2} x=14+\left(-\frac{1}{2} x+\frac{1}{2} x\right)[\because \text { associativity } \\
& \Rightarrow x+5+\frac{1}{2} x=14+0=14 \\
& \Rightarrow x+\frac{1}{2} x+5=14 \quad(\because \text { transitivity }) \\
& \Rightarrow \frac{3}{2} x+(5-5)=14-5=9(\because \text { associativity }) \\
& \Rightarrow \frac{3}{2} x+0=9 \\
& \Rightarrow \frac{3}{2} x=9 \\
& \Rightarrow 2 \cdot \frac{3}{2} x=2 \cdot 9=18 \\
& \Rightarrow 3 x=18 \quad(\because \text { associativity }) \\
& \Rightarrow\left(\frac{1}{3} \cdot 3\right) \cdot x=\frac{1}{3} \cdot 18=6 \\
& \Rightarrow \quad x=6
\end{aligned}
$$
